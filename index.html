About

Karl Horning is a seasoned English teacher who lives in Portugal and teaches students worldwide via the magic of the interwebs.

He studied in London, England and earned a BA in English Literature and an MA in Victorian Studies before absconding in order to train for his CELTA at International House, Lisbon.

In his previous role as a Management Information and Data Analytics Administrator for Kingston University, Karl had a lot of fun creating Visual Basic programs and database queries for academics with little experience of IT.

In his spare time, Karl is a Full Stack Web Development Certification, Computer Software Engineering student at Free Code Camp.
